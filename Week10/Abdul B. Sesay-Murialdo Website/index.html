FACULTY

Techical faculty

Career and technical education (CTE) has traditionally played an important role in Lunsar secondary schools. The first federal law providing funding for vocational education was passed in 1976, even before education was compulsory in every state.

Science faculty

The Mission of the Faculty of Science of the school of Murialdo is to produce highly motivated stusents and postgraduates capable of making a significant contribution towards national development and the well being of mankind, to conduct research and provide advice and consultancy services in various scientific disciplines to foster a better understanding of the environment for sustainable use and conservation of natural resources.

Art faculty

Most of what you learn in art school will be about human visual perception and how to apply your skills to change human perception. How human visual perception is shaped by our biology. You will learn about colour theory, how people preceptive colour, shadow and light.
